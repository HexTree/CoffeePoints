GREEN GROUP

"Less consumption &
waste reduction"

Our mission is to promote and broadcast a zero waste lifestyle, for a more sustainable and happier/healthier community in and outside KAUST by: holding informative and engaging events and workshops; giving away useful and sustainable gadgets with a clear message and purpose; advising and providing convenient and sustainable alternatives to disposables or wasteful routine; improving and promoting the reuse and recycle (reducing consumption prior to recycling); collaborating with campus and community entities; engaging our KAUST scholars and researchers in on-going and enriching dialogue related to the practical and applied uses of science and technology to better our community, our environment and the world.
