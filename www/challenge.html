THE KAUST SUSTAINABILITY CHALLENGE

BE SUSTAINABLE

Avoid using disposable products to reduce pollution and C02 emissions produced throughout their lifecycle.

COLLECT POINTS

Scan the QR code when you bring your own:
- mug to the coffee shop
- bag at the grocery store
- bottle to refill in the water fountains

WIN PRIZES

Bikes, iPads, watersports classes, dinners in KAUST restaurants, shopping vouchers, reusable bottles, bags and mugs.

TELL YOUR FRIENDS

Invite your friends to participate and help spreading the word for a cleaner and safer environment!
