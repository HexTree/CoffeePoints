Help

When you have opted out of wasting a disposable cup, bag or bottle, you can reward yourself with a point for each item you have saved.

- Step 1: Point your camera towards the badge at the site (it should be a mug, bag or bottle icon containing a QR code).

- Step 2: Go to the 'Scan' menu, and scan.

- Step 3: After scanning, you should have received a point, and see your updated totals in the Home menu.

Collect enough points to be eligible for prizes, or entries into the raffle. Stay tuned for more details!
